About Mezza Luna

Warm Italian hospitality, served with care.

Mezza Luna Pasta & Seafood remains your neighborhood Italian table in Marietta: handmade pastas, seafood, wine, cocktails, and service that makes dinner feel personal. Trattoria Bella is the next chapter, carrying the same spirit into a refreshed experience.

  • Current name: Mezza LunaThe dining room, menu, catering, and hospitality you know remain at the center.
  • Coming soon: Trattoria BellaA subtle rebrand inspired by the same family passion and a more elevated evening out.
