Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect customer information such as name, phone number, email address, reservation details, online order details, and customer inquiries.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used only to process reservations, process takeout orders, respond to inquiries, send customer-requested service communications, and improve restaurant operations.

3. SMS Messaging

By calling Mezza Luna Pasta and Seafood and selecting options in our automated phone system, customers may request a reservation link or online ordering link by SMS.

SMS messages are sent only after the customer actively requests them by phone.

Message frequency varies based on customer interaction.

Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to opt out.

Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Mobile Information Non-Sharing

Mobile numbers and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
